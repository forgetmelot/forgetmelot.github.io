Common Sandpiper

Actitis hypoleucos

Small brown sandpiper that bobs its tail constantly. Brown chest patches.

location_on HK, UK
Difficulty to photograph
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Always on the move.
Difficulty to identify
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Small and similar to other sandpipers.

Identification tips

The Green Sandpiper does not bob its tail as much as the Common; the Green also has a white rump while Common has a brown rump.
