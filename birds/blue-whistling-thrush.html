Blue Whistling-Thrush

Myophonus caeruleus

An ultramarine blue bird with silvery dots. Is not actually a thrush (Turdidae). This one is moulting so maybe it's a bit bald. I don't think they are particularly common, so I was lucky to see one in a local park!
