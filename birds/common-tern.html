Common Tern

Sterna hirundo

A white tern with a black cap. Red bill (with a black tip) and legs.

Tail is forked. In flight, note black primaries.

Subspecies longipennis has black bill and legs. In HK, they are not as common as their name advertises.

location_on HK, UK
Difficulty to photograph
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Most often seen flying. Will chase ferries for stirred-up fish.
Difficulty to identify
★ ★ ★ ★ ★
Could be confused with other black-capped terns.

Identification tips

Generally slightly darker than Roseate Tern. In HK, Roseate is the species with red bills and legs (look out for Roseate juveniles with black bills and legs), while Common usually has black bills and legs.
