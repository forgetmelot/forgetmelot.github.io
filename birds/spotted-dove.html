Spotted Dove

Spilopelia chinensis

Most ubiquitous dove in HK. Brown plumage with grey head and spotted "necklace". These guys will build shabby nests on your air-con (external module) and wake you up at 4am.

Perhaps less common, the juvenile has no "necklace".
